George Brown College (GBC) has two campuses, casa loma and st.james. 
GBC offers a variety of programs including but not limited to 
the culinary arts and computer programmer analyst. When one graduates
from GBC they will obtain plenty of hands-on experience to
help prepare them for their future. Not only do GBC Alumni graduate with
plenty of hands on experience, they will also leave with many soft skills
and build relationships and connections.
