George Brown College is a college in Toronto, Ontario. I've been attending this school for 2 years now and im in my last year. The school can offer a lot when it comes to learning stuff in different fields.

There are 3 campus' and each campus has their own specialties. Depending what career you want will decide which campus you attend. The school is known for having a high rates of people getting into a career right after school. The school was founded in 1967.

For example Casa Loma specializes in Engineering and business, the St.James campus is know for their culinary, and the water front campus is known for nursing.
